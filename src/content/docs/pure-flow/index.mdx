## Quick notes

- The profile is based on a direct lever workflow: adjusting pressure to the flow, with a slightly longer pre-infusion.
- Works in time mode and in weight mode.
- Adjust temperature to your bean type. The default is `94°C`.
- Stop weight and/or time also determine the output quantity.
- For `9g` of beans, change the flow value to `1g/s`.
- In weight mode, the pre-infusion phase is also stopped by weight.
